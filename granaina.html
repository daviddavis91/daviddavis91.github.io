Granaína

Granaína (2021), is is a short dance film that explores the contrasts and similarities between Flamenco and the Amsterdam Bijlmer area. The 'Granaína' is a traditional fandango style that originated in Granada. Although Granada, an ancient Spanish city, and the Bijlmer, a gritty looking suburb, are enterily different areas, we observed many interesting similarities.
