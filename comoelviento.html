Como el Viento

Como el Viento (2022), a flamenco x jazz x drill project featuring trumpet player Antonio Moreno Glazkov. The result is a 3-track EP filled with melancholic trumpet lines, dreamy guitar arpeggios, and hard-hitting drums.
